EMO Institutions

  • Orphanage English Medium School Palad

    Orphanage English Medium School is a Co-Educational school affiliated to CBSE syllabus. The school was established in the year 1997 and is managed by Edakkara Muslim Orphanage Committee.

  • MOLP School Munda

    MOLPS Munda Primary School (MMPS) located at Malappuram Nilambur GUPS Marutha Vazhikkadavu is one of the best schools in the town.

  • MOUP School Munda

    MOUPS Munda was established in 1979 and it is managed by the Pvt. Aided. The school consists of Grades from 5 to 7. The school is Co-educational and it doesn't have an attached pre-primary section.

  • EMOR Girls HSS Palad

    EMOR GIRLS HSS PALAD was established in 1995 and it is managed by the Pvt. Aided. The school consists of Grades from 11 to 12. The school is Girls and it doesn't have an attached pre-primary section.
